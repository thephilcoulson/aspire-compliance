## 1. Purpose

To set the standards of ethical behaviour and integrity expected of everyone at Aspire, and to protect the Company, its customers, and its people.

## 2. Scope

All employees, contractors, officers, and directors.

## 3. Core Principles

- **Integrity:** Act honestly and ethically in all dealings.
- **Compliance:** Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and Company policies.
- **Respect:** Maintain a workplace free from harassment and discrimination.
- **Accountability:** Take responsibility for actions and decisions.
- **Customer trust:** Treat customers fairly and protect their data and interests.

## 4. Conflicts of Interest

- Disclose actual or potential conflicts (financial interests, outside roles, related-party dealings).
- Do not let personal interests improperly influence business decisions.

## 5. Anti-Bribery & Corruption

- Zero tolerance for bribery, kickbacks, or improper payments to/from anyone, including public officials.
- Gifts and hospitality must be reasonable, transparent, and within policy limits; record where required.
- Comply with applicable anti-corruption laws (e.g. Singapore PCA, UK Bribery Act, FCPA where relevant).

## 6. Fair Dealing & Competition

- Compete fairly; do not engage in anti-competitive conduct or misuse confidential information of others.

## 7. Confidentiality & Data Protection

- Protect Company, customer, and employee confidential data per the Data Classification (Policy 03) and Privacy (Policy 04) policies.

## 8. Insider Information

- Do not misuse material non-public information for personal gain or share it improperly.

## 9. Protecting Assets

- Use Company assets responsibly and only for legitimate purposes (link Policy 05).

## 10. Anti-Fraud

- Fraud, theft, and falsification of records are prohibited and will be investigated and may be reported to authorities.

## 11. Whistleblowing & Reporting

- Report suspected misconduct via [confidential whistleblowing channel].
- Reports may be made anonymously where permitted.
- **No retaliation** against anyone who reports in good faith.

## 12. Consequences

- Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination and referral to authorities.

## 13. Acknowledgement & Review

All staff acknowledge this Code on hire and at least annually. Reviewed at least annually.
