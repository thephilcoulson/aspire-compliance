## 1. Purpose

To ensure personnel understand and fulfil their security responsibilities before, during, and after employment.

## 2. Scope

All employees, contractors, interns, and temporary staff.

## 3. Before Employment

- **Screening:** Background checks (identity, right-to-work, criminal/financial where lawful and role-appropriate) proportionate to the sensitivity of the role. Enhanced screening for roles with access to Restricted data or financial controls.
- **Terms:** Employment agreements include confidentiality (NDA), security responsibilities, and acceptable use obligations.

## 4. During Employment

- **Onboarding:** Security, privacy, and AML (where relevant) training completed within [first 30 days].
- **Ongoing training:** Annual refresher and role-specific training; phishing simulations.
- **Awareness:** Staff acknowledge policies on hire and after material updates.
- **Disciplinary process:** A formal, fair process applies to security violations.

## 5. Access & Role Changes

- Access is provisioned per the Access Control Policy (Policy 02) and adjusted promptly on role change.

## 6. Termination & Offboarding

- Access revoked within [defined SLA] of departure.
- Return of all Company assets.
- Reminder of continuing confidentiality obligations.
- Exit checklist completed and retained.

## 7. Contractors & Third-Party Personnel

- Subject to equivalent screening, confidentiality, and security obligations, managed under the Vendor Risk Policy (Policy 07).

## 8. Enforcement & Review

Violations may result in disciplinary action. Reviewed at least annually.
